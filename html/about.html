About Us

Awesome Mobile Events

Imagine if you didn't have to worry about having to book a location to throw that event that people raved about for weeks to come. Imagine not having to deal with hiring, cleaning up, making sure the location isn't too far from the guests….and the list goes on and on.

Game Vault parties and events are perfect, as we bring the party to you. Whether you need us at your home, at the local fair, or even at your school, Game Vault will be there on time, and ready to give your guests a memorable experience.

Young and Old

Game Vault don't just do children's birthday parties and school incursions, we also do teenagers parties, gaming events, corporate events, and even bucks nights from time to time. Our friendly, fun and interactive environment makes the perfect event for just about any application. Contact us for your next event

Latest Games, Latest Consoles

At Game Vault, we are passionate about keeping completely up to date with all the latest gaming experiences out there. We regularly update our fleet of games, in all different console formats, including xbox, play station, wii and more. Game Vault parties are kept regularly updated so those from the first time gamer, to the advanced gamer can enjoy their event with Game Vault.

Family Owned, Family Run

Game Vault is proud to be family owned and run. Having children ourselves, we understand how hard it can be to successfully entertain our kids when it comes to birthday events, school holidays and school incursions. Our family values of fun, having a good time, and bringing people together tie directly into every part of our business. We love what we do and we would love to bring the good times to your next event. We give out gift bags after every Game Vault party to ensure that everyone goes home feeling the love!
