Events

Game Vault provides a unique social gaming experience and is perfect for all kinds of events and age groups. Party guests are entertained in a safe and fun environment, and are fully supervised by our professional gaming coach who all have current Working with Children checks.

Game Vault brings the action to you, so you can enjoy a stress free, relaxing experience, while your gamers enjoy the ultimate party experience! All we need is a place to park and people to play.

Our mobile video gaming trucks are available in Perth for all types of events, including:

  • Birthday Parties
  • Vacation Care / OSHC
  • Festivals
  • Sporting Club Events
  • Family Fun Days
  • Corporate Events
  • School Rewards Programs
  • Community/School Fairs
  • School Graduation Parties
  • Weddings
  • School Incursions
  • Gaming Tournaments
  • Fundraising Events
  • Expos
  • Church & Youth Group Programs
