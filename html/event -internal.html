birthdays

The ultimate birthday party idea for kids & teenagers in Perth.

Kids and teenagers of today are no longer satisfied with traditional party games and ice cream cake. They're after a whole new kind of birthday party that's fun, exciting, and different!

Game Vault not only provides kids and teenagers with the ULTIMATE birthday party experience. Adults' birthday parties are also given an injection of sheer fun when hiring us as the concept of gaming is fast becoming the most popular of all party entertainment ideas for kids, teenagers and adults. Our birthday games for adults are guaranteed to bring out the child in you. Our video game birthday party bus is a customised party on wheels that bring gamers an entirely new level of electronic gaming.

Featuring a limousine styled interior, climate controlled interior, massive 3D LED screens, plus a huge selection of legendary multi-player games. If we don't carry a game that you want to play at your party – no problem! We'll do our best to get it for you.

Game Vault also features the very best in gaming sound with Turtle Beach headsets! Our headsets integrates premium stereo game sound with crystal-clear communication to create the definitive audio environment for playing popular game titles on all platforms.

Party hosts can utilise our awesome Bluetooth sound system to play their favourite music and create the perfect party atmosphere. Kids, teenagers and adults alike enjoy every aspect of a video game party, including the fun interior of the van where players are treated to various games that include sports and dancing, in a fun, highlighted environment. When the Game Vault team arrives, you and your guests step into a world of epic gaming adventure for several hours.
